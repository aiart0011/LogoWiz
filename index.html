Privacy Policy - LogoWiz

1. Overview

LogoWiz ("we", "our" or "the Service") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store and disclose your personal information when you use our AI-powered logo design and generation features. By using the Service, you acknowledge that you have read and agree to this Policy and our Terms of Service.

2. Definitions and Key Terms

  • Cookie: Small data files stored in your browser that help identify your browser, provide analytics, remember preferences and login state.
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers and language settings.
  • Personal Information: Data that directly or indirectly identifies a person, such as contact details, brand names, uploaded images, and design preferences.
  • Service: Features provided by LogoWiz including AI logo generation, template customization, project management, and export.
  • You: Individuals or organizations that register and use the Service.

3. Information We Collect

When you access, register or use the Service, we may collect the following categories of information:

  • Information You Provide:
    • Account & Contact: Name, email address (when you register or contact us).
    • Brand & Design Details: Brand name, industry, style keywords (e.g., "modern", "playful"), color preferences, and font choices.
    • Uploaded Materials: Reference images, icons, or other assets you upload for logo inspiration or customization.
    • Prompts & Interactions: Text prompts used for AI generation, style parameters selected, and feedback provided on generated logos.
  • Information Automatically Collected:
    • Device & Usage Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, page response times, session duration, feature usage (e.g., clicks on templates, edits made), and error logs for security and performance analytics.
    • Subscription & Transactions: Records related to purchases or subscriptions, processed by secure third‑party payment platforms.

4. How We Use Information

  • Provide & Personalize the Service: Generate logo concepts based on your input, save your projects and customization history, and offer relevant template and icon suggestions.
  • Model & Product Improvement: Use anonymized and aggregated usage data to improve our AI models, design algorithms, and user experience. Any data used for training is de-identified.
  • Communication: Send important service notices (e.g., account updates, feature changes) and, with your consent, promotional or educational communications about LogoWiz.
  • Security & Compliance: Monitor for fraudulent activity, enforce our terms, and comply with applicable laws and regulations.

5. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted vendors who provide essential services like cloud hosting, data storage, payment processing, and analytics, under strict contractual obligations.
  • Legal Requirements: If required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or government agency).
  • Rights & Safety: To protect the rights, property, or safety of LogoWiz, our users, or the public as required or permitted by law.
  • Corporate Transactions: In connection with a merger, sale of company assets, financing, or acquisition of all or a portion of our business.

6. Data Storage & Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encryption and access controls. We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide the Service, fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or comply with legal obligations.

7. Your Rights

  • Access: You can request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure: You can request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain legal exceptions.
  • Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on your consent, you may withdraw it at any time.

To exercise these rights or for any privacy questions, please contact us at privacy@logowiz.app.

8. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to maintain your session, remember your preferences, and understand how our Service is used. You can control cookie settings through your browser, though disabling them may affect some features.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy regularly.
